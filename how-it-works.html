Container 4 ‚Äî What creates a strong final output

Input framing quality

  • Must include structured memo fields.
  • Must call out missing information explicitly.
  • Must tie debate panel selection to deal context.
  • Must avoid unsupported assumptions.

Output framing quality

  • Persona voices must be distinct and consistent.
  • Debate must respond to prior round arguments.
  • No fabricated numbers or fake precision.
  • Recommendations must be actionable and timed.

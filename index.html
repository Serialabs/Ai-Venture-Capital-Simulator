Analysis Pipeline

01 — Deck Digest

Extract key facts, constraints, and evidence snippets from the deck content.

02 — 10 Persona Reviews

Run ten independent perspectives with clear verdicts, concerns, and crux points.

03 — 5-Round Debate

Bull, Bear, and Wild Card challenge each other with increasing specificity.

04 — Final Synthesis

Publish key insights, founder questions, action plan, and executive summary.

Executive Summary

Lean Pass Strong community narrative but heavy fixed-cost exposure and long-cycle liabilities dominate risk.

Evidence pointers: p.4 membership thesis, p.7 expansion model, p.10 lease exposure.

Key Insights

  • Brand-led demand: messaging and product experience appear differentiated.
  • Financial fragility: occupancy and pricing assumptions are highly sensitive.
  • Scale risk: rapid expansion can hide location-level underperformance.

Founder Questions

  1. What occupancy threshold is required to maintain contribution margin by site?
  2. How resilient is demand through macro downturn scenarios?
  3. What are the earliest signs a location should be paused or exited?

Action Plan

  • Request site-level economics with standardized maturity cohorts.
  • Model downside outcomes for occupancy, churn, and pricing compression.
  • Define strict expansion gates before approving additional locations.

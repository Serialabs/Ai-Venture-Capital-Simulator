Method

AIVC prompt and committee simulation

This page explains the complete process used to produce each report: structured memo extraction, persona analysis, staged debate, and final synthesis output.

4-stage analysis pipeline

01 — Deal Memo

Normalize deck claims into a structured memo with explicit unknowns and risks.

02 — Persona Evaluation

Collect 10 independent investor lenses with verdicts, strengths, and concerns.

03 — IC Debate

Bull, Bear, and Wild Card conduct a five-round rebuttal-driven discussion.

04 — Final Report

Publish key insights, founder questions, an action plan, and an executive summary.

Debate structure

Bull

Argues upside and conditions for category leadership.

Bear

Tests downside paths, weak assumptions, and evidence gaps.

Wild Card

Adds non-obvious constraints and second-order effects.
